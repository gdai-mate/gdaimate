Voice‑to‑Text Reporting

Ever wish you never had to type again? Voice-to-text reporting means you can speak your mind, and your thoughts get structured into presentable, client-facing quotes, notes, invoices, and reports. Simply say "Day 3 of the kitchen renovation, cabinet installation complete" and within seconds you'll have a professional site diary ready to send.

AI Assistant

Imagine saying "Okay mate, generate an invoice for x client for $8,000" and without any work on your behalf, the invoice is sent with payment links and cash gets sent to your pocket. Our AI assistant understands context, handles complex requests, and can even take calls when you're busy on-site.

Interactive Data Chat

Instead of hunting through spreadsheets for information. Just ask "How many more weeks do we have to finish this job?" or "How much have we spent on this site so far?" and get instant, accurate answers with charts and breakdowns—like having a data analyst in your pocket.

Predictive Insights

Get a second, digital brain to enhance quantity surveying and auditing. It helps with walk-throughs and safety checks, reduces human error, and limits costly oversights. The AI detects patterns like "You'll likely face a 3-day weather delay next week, should we rearrange the schedule?"

Real‑Time Dashboards

Transform your voice notes into stunning visual reports that impress clients. At the end of each week, speak your progress update for 2 minutes and our AI automatically generates a professional PDF report with photos, budget updates, and next week's schedule—ready to send.

Safety & Compliance Automation

Stay compliant with all WH&S requirements automatically. Say "AI, create today's toolbox talk for roof work in 35-degree heat" and within minutes you have a customized safety briefing covering heat stress, fall protection, and hydration—ready to deliver to your team.

About Us

G'dai Mate — taking care of the tough stuff.

Founded in 2025 by Queensland entrepreneur Asgard Bishop

G'dai Mate is on a mission to revolutionize how Australia's tradies work. G'dai Mate was born on real job sites where builders were losing nights and weekends to paperwork instead of craftsmanship.

Today, G'dai Mate turns cutting‑edge voice technology and large‑language models into simple user interfaces. Speak your site data once and within seconds it becomes polished quotes, safety docs, real‑time dashboards and invoices that slot straight into the software you already use. No bloated systems - just the information you need, exactly when you need it.

Our mission is clear: give every small tradie team the power of a full‑time data department (without the price tag) so you can get back to building and still make knock‑off. When we're not refining AI pipelines, you'll find us surfing the Sunshine Coast or mentoring founders on ethical automation, because balance matters as much as productivity.

With G'dai Mate taking care of the tough stuff, Australia's tradies can focus on what they do best: building our communities while still enjoying their evenings.
